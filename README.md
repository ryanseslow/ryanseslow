## Live Tools

### MFA Thesis Architect
A modular framework that helps MFA and BFA students structure the core components of their thesis: Abstract, Process, References, Exhibition Simulation, and Reflection. Breaks a 20-page paper down into actionable exercises and questions. Built for studio artists who find traditional academic writing disconnected from their practice. Embedded in the post that gets the most organic search traffic on my site.

**[Try it live](https://www.ryanseslow.com/2026/01/10/how-to-write-your-mfa-thesis-in-fine-art-and-beyond/)**

### AiWebDoc
A free 50+ point diagnostic that tells you whether your website is visible to AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. Full report available for $9.99. (Ok, a bit cliche' but it works and it was my first vibe coded app!)

**[Run a diagnostic](https://aiwebdoc.net)**
